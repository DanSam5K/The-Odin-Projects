Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh thyme
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh oregano
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh rosemary
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh marjoram
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh mint
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh fennel
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh dill
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh thyme
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh oregano
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh rosemary
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh marjoram
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh mint
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh fennel
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh dill
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh thyme
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh oregano
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh rosemary
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh marjoram
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh mint

Instructions Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In a large bowl, mix ground beef, bread crumbs, butter, milk, and grated Parmesan cheese.
  3. In a large skillet, heat butter over medium heat. Add ground beef mixture to skillet and cook until no longer pink, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add remaining ingredients to the skillet and cook until no longer pink, about 5 minutes.
  5. Remove skillet from heat and add to a large baking sheet. Cover with foil and bake until no longer pink, about 30 minutes.
  6. Remove foil and bake until golden brown, about 10 minutes.
  7. Remove from oven and let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
